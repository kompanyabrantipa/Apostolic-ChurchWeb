About Our Church

Learn about our history, mission, and values

Welcome to Apostolic Church International, Louisville Assembly

Founded in 2005, The Apostolic Church International, Louisville Assembly has been a beacon of hope and faith in our community.

Our church family is built on the foundation of apostolic teaching and the transforming power of Jesus Christ. We are a vibrant, diverse, and growing congregation committed to authentic worship, biblical teaching, and compassionate outreach.

Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking for a place to grow spiritually, we welcome you to join us.

Learn About Our Services

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a thriving church that transforms lives, strengthens families, and impacts our community with the love and power of Jesus Christ.

Mission: We exist to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ by creating environments where people are encouraged and equipped to pursue intimacy with God, community with insiders, and influence with outsiders.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do as a church

Authentic Worship

We believe in heartfelt, Spirit-led worship that honors God and transforms lives.

Biblical Teaching

We are committed to teaching the whole counsel of God's Word with clarity and relevance.

Loving Community

We foster genuine relationships where people are loved, accepted, and cared for.

Passionate Service

We serve our church, community, and world with the love of Christ.

Fervent Prayer

We believe prayer is essential and powerful in the life of believers and the church.

Global Mission

We are committed to sharing the Gospel locally and throughout the world.

Spiritual Growth

We encourage continuous growth in faith,character and spiritual maturity through the Word,prayer and fellowship.

Equipping Leaders

We invest in developing leaders who are spiritually grounded and equipped to serve in the church and community.

Our History

The journey of faith that brought us to where we are today

2005

The Beginning

Apostolic Church was established with just 10 founding members meeting in the basement of Senior Minister Kofi Frempong and Lady Sharmette Frempong's home.

2010

First Building

The congregation moved into its first building on Bishop Lane in Southeast Louisville.

2011

New Facility

“New Facility” - Moved to our current location with expanded facilities to accommodate growth.

2023

Mission Expansion

With the help of the Kentucky/Ohio District, the Apostolic Church established the Indianapolis, Indiana Assembly.

Present

Continuing the Legacy

We continue to grow and impact our community with the love of Christ.
